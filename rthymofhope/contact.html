Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our programs and services

How can I donate to support your charity?

You can donate through our website using bank transfer, mobile money (Airtel Money or MTN Mobile Money), or PayPal. Every donation directly supports our educational and community programs in Uganda.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, we are a registered charity in Uganda. All donations are tax-deductible and you will receive a receipt for your contribution.

How is my donation used?

Your donations fund our educational programs, provide scholarships for disadvantaged children, support community development projects, and help us reach more families across Uganda.

Can I sponsor a specific child's education?

Yes! We offer child sponsorship programs where you can directly support a specific child's education, healthcare, and basic needs. Contact us to learn more about available children.

What is the minimum donation amount?

There is no minimum donation amount. Every contribution, no matter how small, makes a difference in transforming lives and communities across Uganda.

How can I volunteer with your organization?

We welcome volunteers for teaching, mentoring, community outreach, and administrative support. Contact us to discuss available opportunities and how you can contribute your skills.
